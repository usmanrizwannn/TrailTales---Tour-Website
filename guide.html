Pakistan Travel Guide

Essential tips for your perfect adventure

Packing Essentials

Layered Clothing

Pakistan's weather varies dramatically. Pack layers for mountains cold and plains hot.

Comfortable Shoes

Trekking shoes for northern areas, sandals for beaches and cities.

Power Bank

Electricity can be unreliable in remote areas.

Water Bottle

Stay hydrated. Carry purification tablets for remote areas.

Budget Planning

  1. Daily Budget: PKR 5,000-15,000 (budget to luxury)
  2. Transport: Book flights/trains early for discounts
  3. Accommodation: Hostels PKR 1,500 to luxury PKR 20,000
  4. Food: Street food PKR 300, restaurants PKR 1,500/meal
  5. Emergency: Keep 20% extra for unexpected costs

Safety First

Travel Advisory

Check latest advisories. Northern areas generally safe for tourists.

Health

Get vaccinations. Carry basic meds + altitude sickness tablets.

Respect Local Customs

Modest clothing outside major cities. Remove shoes entering homes.

Do's

  • Learn basic Urdu phrases
  • Bargain politely at markets
  • Try local tea (chai)
  • Respect prayer times

Don'ts

  • Point feet at people
  • Public displays of affection
  • Photograph military areas
  • Waste food
